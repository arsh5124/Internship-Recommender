About AI Internship Recommender

Built for Smart India Hackathon 2025

Problem Statement

The traditional internship matching process is often manual, time-consuming, and may not result in optimal student-internship pairings. Our AI-powered solution addresses this by providing intelligent, data-driven recommendations.

Our Solution

We've developed a comprehensive AI-based internship recommendation engine that uses:

  • Natural Language Processing (NLP) for skill extraction and analysis
  • Content-based Filtering to match student profiles with internship requirements
  • Collaborative Filtering to leverage community wisdom
  • Hybrid Approach combining multiple algorithms for better accuracy
  • Explainable AI providing clear reasons for recommendations

Key Features

  • Intelligent skill matching
  • Personalized recommendations
  • Skill gap analysis
  • Real-time processing
  • Scalable architecture
  • User-friendly interface
  • Continuous learning
  • Fair and unbiased matching
