About the Project

Smart India Hackathon 2025

Problem Statement

Traditional internship matching systems lack precision and fail to provide personalized recommendations. Students often struggle to find relevant opportunities that align with their skills and career goals, while companies face challenges in identifying the right candidates.

Our Solution

We developed an AI-powered recommendation engine that leverages advanced machine learning algorithms to create precise student-internship matches. Our system uses:

  • Hybrid Filtering: Combines collaborative and content-based filtering
  • NLP Processing: Analyzes job descriptions and student profiles
  • Explainable AI: Provides transparent reasoning for recommendations
  • Skill Gap Analysis: Identifies learning opportunities
